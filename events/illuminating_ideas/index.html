Illuminating Ideas

    Spark creativity and innovation at 'Illuminating Ideas' - an event dedicated to unveiling exciting startup ventures and fostering entrepreneurial spirit!

    Registration Closes on 10th October - 11:59PM!

    Date : 14th October

    Time : 10:00 AM ~ 12:00 PM





    This is a Team event.

    Guidelines



  • Either individual or 2 participants is allowed

  • No larger teams are allowed

  • Each team will be allotted a maximum of 5 minutes to present their startup idea.

  • Time will be strictly enforced, and presentations exceeding this limit may face penalties.


    • We highly recommend that you follow these steps in your presentation:


  • The first page of your presentation should prominently display the title of your startup idea and the names of the presenters.

  • Ensure clarity and professionalism in your presentation cover page.

  • All content and materials presented must adhere to ethical standards and respect the event's guidelines.

  • Any inappropriate or offensive content will not be tolerated.



      • Audience Interaction:


  • After each presentation, there may be a brief Q&A or feedback session with the audience or judges."

  • Participants should be prepared to engage in constructive discussions about their startup ideas.

  • Being open to feedback and actively participating in these discussions can greatly enhance the quality and potential of your startup concept.



      • We are eagerly waiting to see you all in the event.
